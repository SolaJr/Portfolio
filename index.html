Balloon Grid

Role - 

Mechanical Engineering Research Assistant

Modern architecture has been moving away from concrete buildings due to high expences associated with complex designs. However, since most of the cost for a building comes from the labors developing it, we decided to create a press that could simplify the process of making concrete roofs. So we developed a multipoint actuator to simplify the process of making presses for the concrete. That way, instead of wasting thousands of dollars on wood and materials to build frames, our Balloon Grid press can form them with a single person. If you would like to speak about this project more, please reach out to me since I can't say too much publicly.
